Singapore

In the small but bustling island nation of Singapore, you'll find a fusion of Chinese, Malaysian and Indian cultures. The city's original name, Singapura (Lion City), comes from the Malay legend of a lion that fought off an attack from an evil deity. It became a British trading post in 1819 and was ceded to the British Empire in 1824.
